Bradley Murrell

Dog Trainer / Dog Walker / Dog Sitter

My name is Brad and I'm from New Zealand. I grew up in the countryside and had dogs beside me my whole life.

Before coming to Sweden, I spent many years traveling Europe where I have done jobs with dogs including volunteer work in shelters and at a dog hotel in the Swiss mountains.

I came to Sweden as a dog handler to take care of a very special dog. I fell in love with Sweden and decided to stay.

Since living in Sweden, I have become a professional dog walker working for Tassevärld in Stockholm. I have also become a qualified dog trainer and train dogs in my spare time as well as dog sitting.
